Our Group

The construction business of Julius Berger is the heart of the Julius Berger Group. Subsidiaries contribute to this core business, making it possible to offer a broad range of products and services. This structure allows Julius Berger to effectively manage and fulfill construction projects, starting from the initial idea, through to design and engineering, construction and maintenance.

Julius Berger International GmbH (JBI), in Germany, serves as a key collaborator in planning, design and engineering by unlocking expansive knowledge on cutting-edge construction technologies worldwide. In addition, JBI logisticians guarantee global procurement of materials that cannot be sourced and supplied locally in Nigeria.

Primetech Design and Engineering Nigeria Ltd. provides design and engineering for projects in the oil and gas sector. The company works in compliance with the Nigerian Oil and Gas Industry Content Development Act and has the capabilities to provide (pre-) feasibility studies and front end engineering design (FEED).

Julius Berger Services Nigeria Ltd. serves as a multiservice terminal operator at the Warri Old Port. The company employs a comprehensive equipment pool and vast terminal size, which together ensure timely and efficient loading, offloading and storage. Services include: stevedoring, shipping, warehousing and slipway.

Julius Berger Medical Services Ltd. (JBMS) acts as the medical service provider for all company employees and their families. JBMS employs numerous medical doctors, nurses, medical laboratory and radiology technicians, pharmacists and additional auxiliary staff.

Abumet Nigeria Ltd. is leading aluminum manufacturer in Nigeria. The company produces a range of aluminum products including windows, doors, curtain walls, roofing and facades which are used by Julius Berger in the turnkey construction of buildings throughout the country.

Julius Berger Investments Limited serves for the planning and support activities of the Strategic Business Development.
