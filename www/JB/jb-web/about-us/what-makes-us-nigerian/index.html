What makes us Nigerian?

While Vision 2020 will not come into being overnight, it gives a clear focus for our way forward. As exemplified by the past 45-plus years of our work, Julius Berger is fully committed to supporting the realization of Nigeria’s goals for modernization whenever possible.

Indeed, we have already played an integral part in helping lay the foundations for the country's development and progress through the construction of essential infrastructural and traffic networks. Furthermore, JB is pioneering the use of innovative technologies and driving the transfer of this knowledge into Nigeria through a cooperative relationship with NOTAP, the National Office for Technology Acquisition and Promotion. 

We are committed to investing in our diverse workforce, 96% of which is made up of Nigerian staff members. Our strong emphasis on training and continuing education results in the long-term staff employment as well as the personal and professional development of employees, covering a very large number of different professions. We take great pride knowing that numerous people have directly or indirectly benefited from the employment Julius Berger provides. 

Our goal is to ensure that our contribution to transportation, planning and knowledge transfer helps facilitate the country meeting its goals for improvement. Following in the footsteps of our first project, we want to help build the bridge between vision and reality for Nigeria in the 21st century.
