Josefine Erles: Snapshot of my internship at Julius Berger

I’m Josefine Erles from Rhineland Palatinate in Germany. From May to July 2011 I did an internship at Julius Berger, in the Corporate Communications Department of the company. I rapidly got my own project in the content of JB’s Corporate Social Responsibility initiative and was able to complete almost all of my ideas. I could always find a colleague who was eager to help me whenever I had any challenges. I was involved in every part of the department and even got the chance to go to Lagos and see more of the company’s competences and also more of the country which is really fascinating. 

Working with JB did not only offer me the chance to gather a lot of experiences for my later professional life but it also gave me the chance to work together with people from different nations of the world.  

In the company there is a special atmosphere among colleagues, which made it easy for me to integrate into the department and the company. My experience working with JB was rewarding, and I can only advise everybody to do the same. JB offers opportunities for all kinds of people with all different talents.
