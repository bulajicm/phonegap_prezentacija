Julius Berger Donates 1,000 books to Public Library

Julius Berger Nigeria PLC, in continuation of its Literacy Campaign has made a donation of One thousand (1,000) books to the Lagos State Government Library at Sabo, Yaba, on May 28, 2013

This is in continuation of Julius Berger’s Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR), initiative that has been ongoing for more than one year. It is Julius Berger’s token intervention in rekindling and enhancing reading and writing skills among students in secondary schools across the country. The book donation is part of the book drive from the company´s literacy campaign that saw families, individuals and corporate bodies donating books to the initiative. More of the donated books will be distributed to libraries and schools in other states over time.
