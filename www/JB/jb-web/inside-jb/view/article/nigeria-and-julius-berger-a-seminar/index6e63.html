Nigeria and Julius Berger – A Seminar

Recently in Abuja, a newly constructed meeting room was filled with more than 50 people when Managing Director Wolfgang Goetsch held his opening speech for a seminar titled “Nigeria and Julius Berger”.

A group of young employees, all participants of the Nachwuchskräfte-Entwicklungsprogramm (a joint development program for young professionals of JB and BB Nigeria) organized the seminar and invited other young colleagues from all over Nigeria, as well as the JB Management Staff to attend.

The audience participated in seven interesting lectures from internal and external experts on Nigeria, its economic and on political development. Further, presentations were made on the future outlook and prospects for the Julius Berger. Emphasized as part of the vivid discussions was the importance of gaining broader perspectives on the country and the company. By the end of the seminar all in attendance agreed upon one thing: that such events should take place more often and regularly.
