Business Development

As a leader in the Nigerian construction industry and a multi-service partner, customer satisfaction is at the heart of Julius Berger’s business development strategy. JB strives to build enduring partnerships based on mutual trust, respect and a fair return for the value we deliver.
 
 While profitability is not the only gauge of a company’s success, it remains an important one. Year after year Julius Berger has provided its shareholders with a fair return on their investment. While the global economy is facing severe breakdowns, Nigeria has navigated its way through difficult times with minimal impact to the economy or the development plans of the nation. This has allowed the Federal Government and the private sector to continue with their budgeted project investment plans, and Julius Berger benefited accordingly, maintaining a full project portfolio.

Julius Berger remains a leader through dedication, and investment in continuing education and a pledge to quality. Each and every day there are challenges to the continued success of our company and it is only through effective risk assessment, proactive management and efforts of many individuals that the company continues to prosper.

Julius Berger is confident that its business model is fit for the times. Nevertheless, the company will continually analyze and critique its strategies and, where necessary, adjust objectives to accommodate changes in the business environment.
