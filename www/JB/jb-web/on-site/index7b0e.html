Highlighted Projects

Abuja Airport Expressway

Abuja Airport Expressway is being expanded to ten lanes plus service roads including two cloverleaf junctions. Julius Berger will complete this massive project in record time thanks to a team of highly qualified employees and a comprehensive fleet of machinery and equipment.

Admiralty Alexander (Lekki-Ikoyi) Link Bridge, Lagos

The Admiralty Alexander (Lekki-Ikoyi) Link Bridge connects two highly frequented districts of Lagos, providing relief to existing routes in the economic center of Nigeria. With impressive pylon and cable-stayed construction, technology never before used in Nigeria, Julius Berger’s pioneering role in technological development is showcased.

Infrastructural Works, Akwa Ibom

In Uyo, the capital of Akwa Ibom, Julius Berger is responsible for the construction of several regional roads and flyovers easing traffic in the city center. Also developed are two large-scale drainage projects, relieving entire districts from incessant flooding. To complete the projects, Julius Berger utilized closed pipe jacking, a construction method that is more efficient and environmentally-friendly than open wastewater canals. A technique that Julius Berger pioneered in Nigeria.

 

Central Bank of Nigeria, Lagos

Julius Berger is currently rebuilding the Central Bank of Nigeria Branch Office in Lagos, after having successfully constructed the Central Bank’s Headquarters in Abuja. Further to solving the logistical challenges of working in the center of the tightly populated mega-city, geotechnical experts carried out comprehensive subsoil investigations in order to determine adequate pile construction before works for the 100m building could start.

Bonny Island Gas Liquefaction Plant

For one of the world’s largest gas liquefaction plants, located on Bonny Island, Julius Berger has been the partner of choice for civil engineering. Since the beginning of the project in 1996, Julius Berger has handed over more than 35 different orders to the full satisfaction of the client. Currently works include development of the Bonny Airstrip.

Escravos Gas-to-Liquids Plant

Julius Berger participated as the principal civil engineering contractor for the Chevron Gas-to-Liquids Plant in Escravos. Julius Berger constructed, amongst other works, a specialized wharf that made delivery and unloading of two 2,200 ton reactors possilble. Throughout the lifetime of the project, a record 14.4 million man-hours without accidents or injuries was achieved.
