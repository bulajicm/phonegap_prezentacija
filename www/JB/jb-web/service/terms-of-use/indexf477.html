Fraud Alert
Beware of fraudulent activities. There is only one Julius Berger Nigeria, but there are various persons/entities plagiarizing the information of this company. Such scammers may use the company’s logo and profile including: facts, historical information, pictures, names of employees and even the entire contents of our website.

Spot fraudulent activity by using these tips:

  • Check the address. Julius Berger is a Nigerian company that operates only in Nigeria.

  • Check the logo. The Julius Berger logo is a legally protected trademark of Julius Berger Nigeria Plc. Any other company using this logo is doing so illegally and should not be trusted.

  • Check the contact name. Google search the contact person’s name. Many times you will find it is the name of a public figure or that there is a history of scammers using the same name.

  • Check the domain name. All authentic Julius Berger communications will come from the domain name: julius-berger.com

  • Check the request. If the request involves collecting/receiving money from someone you have never met, then it is likely a scam. Julius Berger does not require payment of any fees for any reason whatsoever.

  • Know the Facts. There are many scam artists targeting job seekers. Know that Julius Berger does not recruit employees via email or internet or any other online job recruiting platforms.
