Corporate Citizenship

Julius Berger is dedicated to impacting sustainable progress in Nigeria through corporate social responsibility. The company believes its duty, apart from financial success, is to support and advance the development of the country in the interest of its people.

  • Julius Berger actively seeks opportunities to create measurable differences in the future of people and communities. Key areas of focus include health, education and sports.

  • Julius Berger offers continuous professional development programs, such as on-the-job training and mentoring programs, which highlight the focused and goal oriented investment in employee education.

  • The company pioneers efforts that create positive momentum in the area of environmental protection and development.

  • The company provides support when social services are scarce or have reached the limits of their capacities, including emergency response, civil protection or fire service.

NIBUCAA
