Health, Safety and Environment

Health, Safety and Environment (HSE) is a fundamental component of Julius Berger’s business principles and corporate culture. The company believes that effective management of HSE affairs not only protects staff, but all so contributes to the company’s success.

  • Monitoring and control procedures including site inspections and audits, regular safety meetings and targeted educational campaigns support implementation of the HSE policy and ensure compliance to set guidelines.

  •  Julius Berger invests in the ongoing training of its workforce at all levels within the organization, with the goal of shaping an accident free environment.

  • Julius Berger operates under the strictest conditions and controls for oil and gas consortiums; the company has an impressive record integrating the distinctive regulatory demands of the oil and gas industry into existing HSE processes and procedures.

  • In-house medical services provide comprehensive healthcare to all Julius Berger staff and their families.  

  • The company recycles whenever possible and works to ensure raw materials and energy are used efficiently; Julius Berger exceeds local waste management guidelines.

Our HSE Goals

1. Achieve zero fatalities.
2. Reduce the Lost Time Injury (LTI) frequency
    rate.
3. Minimize damage to the environment by
    implementing ISO 14001 Environment 
    Management System.
