Solutions Provider

From concept to construction and maintenance, Julius Berger offers optimized and tailored solutions to project challenges and conditions, ensuring work is done on-time and according to client requirements.

  • Julius Berger works with a holistic, solution driven mindset; the company integrates its multiple resources to offers clients complete construction related services.

  • The company has established the necessary infrastructure for the mobilization of resources and carrying out of work on a wide range of construction projects, regardless of location in Nigeria.

  • Julius Berger has built a seamless logistics chain to ensure procurement needs are met; international procurement is done by the company‘s subsidiary Julius Berger International.

  • Turnkey construction is made possible through the company‘s own furniture production facility, AFP.  Utilizing state-of-the-art machinery and computerized equipment, a full line of high quality furniture can be produced.
